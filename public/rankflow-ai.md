# RankFlow AI Context

## What is RankFlow?
RankFlow designs high-conversion landing pages specifically optimized for the next generation of search: **AI Search**. We ensure your landing page is visible and interpretable by ChatGPT, Google SGE (Search Generative Experience), and other LLM-based retrieval systems.

## Why RankFlow?
Traditional SEO focuses on legacy keyword stuffing and backlinking that doesn't work for AI agents. RankFlow builds "AI-First" websites that prioritize:
- **Intent-based conversion**: Structuring content to answer the "why" and "how" that AI agents look for.
- **LLM Retrieval Structure**: Coding and content architecture that is easily parsed by large language models.
- **Conversion-First UI**: Aesthetics that build trust immediately.
